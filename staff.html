Meet Our Staff Team

The dedicated individuals who work hard to make RavenMC a fun, fair, and friendly environment for all players.

Our Team

The RavenMC staff team is comprised of dedicated volunteers who are passionate about creating an amazing Minecraft experience for our community. Each staff member undergoes a rigorous application and training process to ensure they have the skills and character needed to represent our server.

Our team works around the clock to provide server maintenance, player support, event organization, and rule enforcement. If you ever need assistance or have questions, don't hesitate to reach out to any of our staff members!

Join Our Staff Team

We're always looking for dedicated and passionate players to join our staff team. If you love RavenMC and want to help make it an even better place for everyone, consider applying for a staff position!

Requirements

  • Active player for at least 1 month
  • No previous rule violations
  • Mature and respectful attitude
  • Ability to communicate clearly in English
  • Willingness to volunteer at least 5 hours per week
